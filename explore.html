Explore NYC Public Middle Schools

Here you can explore the different school districts and boroughs. In the left side of the maps you can choose which variable you want to explore. The color of an area indicates the average of the chosen variable for that specific area compared to the other areas. The colorscheme from low to high is shown in the top right corner. Furthermore, you can hover over an area to see the exact numbers for that area. You can also zoom in and explore which schools are located in each area by clicking on the map. You can change the color mode of the background maps if you have a preference - this is done in the bottom of the left hand side.
But first, we ask you to please be patient while the maps load. We recommend a cup of coffee
